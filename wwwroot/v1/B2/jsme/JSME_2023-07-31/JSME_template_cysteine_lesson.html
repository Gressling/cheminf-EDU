Template Cysteine lesson

This example shows how to build a simple lesson to learn the making of the S-S bond

Cystine is a sulfur-containing amino acid obtained by the oxidation of two cysteine molecules which are then linked via a disulfide bond.

Combine two cysteine molecules to make up a cystine molecule
Procedure:
  1. click on the sulfur atom of the cysteine in the template (below the editor)t to mark the atom to be used as an anchor
  2. click on the editor to add the cysteine
  3. click on the sulfur atom of the cysteine in the editor
